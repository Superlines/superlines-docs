Superlines is an **AI Search Visibility** platform that monitors how your brand appears in AI-powered search results — and gives you actionable insights to improve it.

As AI models like ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews increasingly replace traditional search, Superlines ensures your brand stays visible and competitive in this new landscape.

## What does Superlines do?

| Capability | What it means |
|---|---|
| **AI Search Monitoring** | Track how often your brand is mentioned in responses from ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Claude, Grok, Mistral, and DeepSeek. |
| **Competitor Benchmarking** | See how your Share of Voice compares to competitors across every LLM. |
| **Citation Tracking** | Know when and where your website is cited as a source in AI responses. |
| **Content Opportunities** | Discover topics and queries where you can improve your AI visibility. |
| **Prompt Management** | Track the questions your audience asks AI — and measure your performance on each one. |
| **Team Collaboration** | Role-based access so your whole team can monitor and act. |

## Who is it for?

Superlines is built for teams who need to understand and optimize their brand's presence in the new AI-driven search landscape:

- **Marketing & brand teams** — track brand visibility, sentiment, and share of voice across AI engines
- **SEO professionals** — optimize content for AI citations and fan-out queries alongside traditional search
- **Content & comms teams** — identify content gaps and monitor how AI represents your messaging
- **Agencies** — manage AI search visibility for multiple clients with separate brands, shared reporting, and scalable workflows via API and MCP
- **PR & insights teams** — monitor brand sentiment in AI responses and track competitor positioning

Whether you're an enterprise brand team managing visibility across brand, content, comms, and PR teams — or a performance-focused marketer targeting high-value AI search prompts — or an agency managing multiple clients — Superlines adapts to your workflow. See [Use Cases](/getting-started/use-cases) for detailed examples.
