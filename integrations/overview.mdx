## How integrations work

Integrations in Superlines are configured **per brand**. When you connect an external service, you choose which brand the data flows into. This means you can connect different Google Analytics properties to different brands.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a brand">
    Navigate to **Integrations** and make sure the correct brand is selected in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a service">
    Click **Connect** on the integration card and complete the OAuth authorization flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose properties">
    For Google integrations, select the specific property or site you want linked to the brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using enriched data">
    Connected data appears automatically in your dashboards, metrics, and reports.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Permissions

Only **Owners** and **Admins** can manage integrations. Team members with the **Member** role are redirected away from the Integrations page.

<Info>
You can disconnect any integration at any time from the Integrations page without losing your historical Superlines data.
</Info>
