Superlines integrates with **Google Looker Studio** (formerly Google Data Studio), so you can build custom dashboards and reports that combine AI search visibility data with your other marketing data.

## What you can do

- **Custom dashboards** — build branded reports with your Superlines data
- **Cross-platform reporting** — combine AI visibility data with Google Analytics, Search Console, and more
- **Automated reports** — schedule email delivery of dashboards to stakeholders
- **Team sharing** — share interactive dashboards with your team

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your API key">
    Go to **Organization Settings → API Keys** and generate a key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Looker Studio">
    Navigate to [lookerstudio.google.com](https://lookerstudio.google.com)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a data source">
    Click **Create → Data Source** and search for the **Superlines** connector.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Enter your API key when prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your brand">
    Choose the brand you want to visualize.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your report">
    Drag and drop Superlines metrics into charts, tables, and scorecards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available data fields

| Field category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Metrics** | Brand Visibility, SOV, Citation Rate, Mentions, Sentiment |
| **Dimensions** | AI Engine, Date, Prompt, Label, Country, Language |
| **Competitor data** | Competitor names, competitor visibility, competitor citations |
| **Citation data** | Cited domains, cited URLs, citation counts |
