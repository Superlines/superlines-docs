Stay up to date with the latest changes to Superlines. We ship improvements regularly — this page highlights the most significant updates.

## February 2026

### Content Optimization (Beta)
Enhance existing content to boost AI visibility. Upload or import your content and receive AI-powered suggestions for improvement. Available in the [Content Module](/content/overview).

### Cross-Brand Analytics
Compare AI search performance across multiple brands and domains in a single view. Includes domain performance tables, prompt opportunities, trend comparisons, and advanced filters. See the [Cross-Brand Analytics guide](/analytics/cross-brand-analytics).

### Action Center
A new page for tracking AI-generated recommendations. Actions are organized by category (content, technical, schema) with status tracking. See the [Action Center guide](/features/action-center).

### AI Agent improvements
The conversational AI assistant now supports more complex multi-step analyses, competitor deep-dives, and generates actionable recommendations that appear in the Action Center.

---

## January 2026

### Schema Optimiser
Analyze any URL for Schema.org structured data and generate AI-optimized JSON-LD ready to implement. See the [Schema Optimiser guide](/tools/schema-optimiser).

### Brand Sentiment by theme
The Brand Sentiment page now breaks down sentiment by conversation themes, showing how your brand is perceived across different topic areas.

### Keyword Research tool
A new SEO tool for discovering keywords with search volume, CPC, intent, and trend data. Available in the Tools section — see the [Keyword Research guide](/tools/keyword-research).

---

## December 2025

### Site Analyzer
Full technical SEO audit tool with issue categorization, priority fixes, and mobile/performance checks. See the [Site Analyzer guide](/tools/site-analyzer).

### AI Search Checker
Evaluate any webpage for AI search readiness across five dimensions: SEO metadata, structured data, semantic HTML5, content quality, and LLM readiness. See the [AI Search Checker guide](/tools/ai-search-checker).

### Looker Studio connector
Connect Superlines data to Google Looker Studio for custom dashboards and automated reporting. See the [Looker Studio integration guide](/integrations/looker-studio).

---

## November 2025

### Content Generation
Create AI-optimized content using templates designed for Answer Engine Optimization. Multiple template categories: informational, comparative, social, and transactional. See the [Content Module guide](/content/overview).

### GA4 AI Traffic
Track traffic from AI platforms to your website using Google Analytics 4 data. Includes landing page analysis, geographic breakdown, and platform performance. See the [GA4 AI Traffic guide](/website/ga4-ai-traffic).

### Export Report Builder
Generate customizable PDF reports from the Visibility Dashboard with white-label branding and shareable URLs. See the [Export & Reporting guide](/features/export-reporting).

## Earlier releases

### Site Health monitoring
Monitor your website's technical health and AEO readiness with per-page analysis, health scores, and issue tracking.
